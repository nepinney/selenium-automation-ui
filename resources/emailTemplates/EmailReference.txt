Stucture
---: breaks one section from another
ex:
    Subject
---
    Body
---End (last one marks the end of the email)

<tsc>: the tsc number of the ticket
<owner>: the owner of the device to be returned
<model>: the model of the machine if given in ticket
<sn>: the serial number of the device
<address>: the client to update and validate from the ticket
<device>: the type of machine being returned (laptop, desktop) starting with a uppercase
<deviceL>: the type of machine being returned (laptop, desktop) starting with a lowercase
